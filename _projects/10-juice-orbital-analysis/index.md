# Jupiter Icy Moons Explorer Investigation

Abdelrahman Al Naqeeb 202200281  
March 2025

## Abstract

This report presents an investigation of the European Space Agency’s JUICE (Jupiter Icy Moons Explorer) mission from the perspective of orbital and space flight mechanics. Emphasis is placed on its complex gravity assist trajectory, orbital insertion at Jupiter, and final orbit around Ganymede.
